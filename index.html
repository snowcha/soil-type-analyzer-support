Privacy Policy

Soil Type Analyzer does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app works entirely on your device. Photos you choose or take, and the measurements you enter, stay on your device and are never uploaded to any server. The app has no account system, no analytics, and no ads.

Because no data leaves your device, no data is collected by the developer or any third party.
